Ethical design · anti-doomscroll feed

A feed that helps you leave in a better mood than you arrived.

This social feed concept adds friction on purpose: a breathing ritual instead of yanking-to-refresh, transparent ranking instead of hidden signals, and softer exit paths before the session turns into burnout.

Friction + transparency

Breathe-to-refresh as a humane interrupt

The product inserts a tiny ritual between desire and novelty, turning “refresh” into a conscious action rather than a compulsive twitch.

Friction-positive pattern
  • Instead of: instant pull-to-refresh and endless novelty.
  • Use: a deliberate breath hold that slows the impulse loop.
  • Outcome: the feed feels less slot-machine-like and more reflective.
  • Fail-safe: releasing early cancels the refresh and keeps the current context intact.

Feed nutrition labels, not black-box ranking

Every ranked post exposes the signals that brought it into the feed so the user can understand and critique the algorithm.

Nutrition label anatomy
Topic dwellWhat themes you have been staring at for too long.
Social proximityWhether friends, creators, or the wider network influenced the ranking.
NoveltyWhether this post expands your world or simply reinforces a loop.
Wellbeing overrideWhen the system intentionally slows the feed.
