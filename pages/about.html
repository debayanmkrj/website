ABOUT ME

Hi, Thank you for visiting my website. I am Debayan Mukherjee, a creative technologist with over 10 years of experience bridging the gap between artistic expression and engineering scale. Currently pursuing an MS in Media Arts and Sciences at Arizona State University, with a concentration in multi-modal generative art, I specialize in designing human-centered workflows that leverage both technical and creative aesthetics.

My professional journey spans from hands-on photography and design to leading global creative teams at Amazon, where I managed cross-functional teams across Middle East, Africa, Turkey, India, and Singapore markets. This trajectory has given me experience in both the creative process and the scalable production systems needed to deliver visual content at enterprise scale.

My work focuses on the intersection of generative AI, computer vision, interactive media, and workflow automation. I develop immersive experiences using real-time interactive systems while optimizing production pipelines for efficiency and quality.
